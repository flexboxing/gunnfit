ONLINE PERSONAL TRAINING

Your health is an investment, not an expense.

HOW IT WORKS

We will conduct an initial call via phone or Skype/FaceTime to determine what your goals are and how we can best work together to meet them.

Based on the information I gather about you - how much time you can spend working out each day, experience in the gym, diet, etc - I will put together a week long workout routine for you to begin. I will also provide you with nutritional advice to follow as you change your activity level.

At the end of the first week, we will have another meeting to see how the first week went as well as address any changes you would like to see in your routine moving forward. Based off the changes you request, I will then put together another three weeks worth of workouts for you to complete the first month.

I also create a Google doc which we will both have access to and use to post your workouts and track your progress.

WHY CHOOSE GUNNFIT?

Personally, I believe every workout routine works if they are designed properly and adhered to. There are thousands of certified (as well as uncertified) trainers who know how to design incredible workout programs. What makes me different is that my top priority is creating a true shift in each client’s mindset. Anyone can be motivated to take their health and fitness to the next level, but I work to instill in my clients the discipline to stay the course even when motivation wanes. I want to see you succeed as badly as you want to succeed, and I won’t settle for anything less.

I look forward to the potential opportunity to work with you and guiding you on your journey to a healthier and fitter life.
