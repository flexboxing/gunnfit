ONLINE PERSONAL TRAINING

HOW IT WORKS

We will conduct initial phone call or Skype/FaceTime to determine your goals are and how we can best work together to meet them.

Based on the information I gather about you - how much time you can spend working out each day, experience in the gym, diet, etc - I will put together a week long workout routine for you to begin, as well as offer nutritional advice to follow as you initially change your activity level.

At the end of the first week, we will have another meeting to catch up and see how the first week went as well as address any changes you would like to see in your routine moving forward. Based off the changes you would like to see, I would then design another three weeks of workouts for you to follow to complete the first month.

Nutritional consultations are conducted via Skype/FaceTime or phone call so I can collect information to determine what will benefit you best.
